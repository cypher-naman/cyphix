Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

FitSensei collects information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, update your profile, or contact us for support. This includes:

  • Personal information (name, email address, date of birth)
  • Fitness data (workout history, health metrics, progress tracking)
  • Device information (device type, operating system version)
  • Usage information (app features used, time spent in app)

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our fitness tracking services
  • Personalize your workout recommendations using AI
  • Send you important updates about your account and the app
  • Respond to your questions and provide customer support
  • Analyze app usage to enhance user experience

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Your fitness data is encrypted and stored securely on our servers.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except as described in this policy. We may share aggregated, non-identifiable information for research and analytics purposes.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information at any time through your account settings. You may also contact us directly for assistance with your data rights.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at fitsensei@cyphix.org
